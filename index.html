About The School


Shri Ram Centennial School is a co-ed Boarding and Day Boarding institution situated in the very scenic Dehradun Valley. It draws on the very best of modern educational practices. Excellence in academics is complemented with a plethora of co-curricular activities and we aim to develop young people who are visionary, articulate, equipped to achieve their full potential in leadership and life and have a global outlook.

The vision statement of the school states that ‘the community will be enhanced through a variety of social, recreational, cultural activities, adventure sports, community service to ensure the overall growth of the young minds.’ It is our endeavor at Shri Ram Centennial School, Dehradun to provide stimulating opportunities where we create Global Citizens who understand that love and compassion are complimentary to each other and are the greatest possession. Our curriculum draws on the very best of modern educational practices where students are exposed to a wide range of cultures and activities where they think and act globally. Our aim is to develop young people who are visionary, articulate and ethical equipped to achieve their full potential in leadership and life. International dimensions help foster and augment the 7C’s that our school stand for i.e. Creativity, Critical thinking, Collaboration, Confidence, Curiosity, Compassion and Commitment.

About Spark

"Every child has a spark. SRCS Spark contest offers the challenge to tap it!"

Our vision from the very beginning has remained the same: to develop our students holistically and to discover their intrinsic creativity and ignite this spark. A spark is the first step to kindle the fire within. Organised by Shri Ram Centennial School every year, the Spark Contest is a wonderful opportunity for those participants who have the fiery aspirations in them to ignite their "Creative Spark" and to exhibit their talents.

What started in 2016, as an Inter-School contest with merely four events, Spark has now advanced to a national level multicultural event with over twenty components, catering to all age groups.

Spark has reigned in every SRCians’ heart since its inception. The whole school descends into a boisterous environment on this day, with many schools visiting our campus ready to prove their mettle. Enthusiastic participants, loud cheering by the audience, the last minute at times loud warm-ups bring the 25- acre campus to life. This competition does not only act as a platform to showcase their competitive spirits, but it also provides them with an opportunity to spark new friendships.

Unison World School, Dehradun was the recipient of the Spark Overall Trophy in 2019. However, Spark Contest 2020 could not be held because of the Covid pandemic but now we are back with Spark Contest, 2021.

Spark Contest 2021 has all events designed and managed by the students, for the students, fostering in them entrepreneurial culture and ethical leadership.

A spark in human history revolutionised human life!

Come discover the fire within you!

GENERAL GUIDELINES FOR SPARK CONTEST 2021:
  • All events will be conducted virtually
  • Participants must register through the Spark Contest 2021 Website Registration link latest by 30th July, 2021.
  • There is no entry fee for the competition
  • The participants are requested to submit their entries (videos, audios, photographs) as per the date and time given for each event. Failing to meet the deadline will result in disqualification.
  • The submissions/file names must include the event number, name of the participant and the school.
  • All submissions must be made in the following format:
    Video: .mp4
    [Video quality - 720p. Screen size - 16:9 (landscape/ full screen). File size (per performance) must not exceed more than 200MB and therefore needs to be accordingly reduced through an editing software.]
    Audio: .mp3
    Photographs: .jpeg
  • The mode of submission should be any one of the following: One Drive, Google Drive or We transfer. All submission links must be collated and sent together to the host school only once in a single email to: srcsspark21@gmail.com

  • Dress code for Online events scheduled for 13th August 2021: School Formals
  • The Result will be published on the Spark Contest 2021 Website on 14th August 2021.
  • Position Holders will be conferred with e-certificates.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
  • The host school will not be held responsible for any technical difficulties that might arise at the participant’s end during online zoom events.
